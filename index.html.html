Mr. J.Sampath Kumar


Mr. J.Sampath Kumar who had started his career as a civil engineer was drawn toward the wonders of Abacus and its potential to unlock remarkable abilities in children. He was the founder of Virtuoso Academy and had been into skill development for more than 2 decades. He is a visionary, a man with big dreams and an optimist who has always strived for the betterment of not just himself but also for the people around him. He has paved a new path of light especially for women and children. Virtuoso Academy was started with just one motive, to give a better future and the opportunity for children & women that they truly deserve. Children struggling with learning difficulties were given the love and attention they were deprived of and were taught a key set of skills to cope with and prove themselves to the world. Educated unemployed women with passion for teaching were given an opportunity to be a part in making a child’s life better and a platform to create an identity for themselves. Today Virtuoso Academy has collectively grown as a family and each one share a unique bond with each other and is strongly motivated with this burning desire to create a positive impact in one’s life.

ABOUT THE PROGRAM

We are glad to introduce ourselves as Virtuoso Academy, pioneers in skill development programs specialised in Abacus & Mental Arithmetic concepts.
“Virtuoso Abacus & Mental Arithmetic” program is a system of education that optimizes the potential of the child’s brain and helps to enhance the learning ability, observation, concentration, retention and recall.
Abacus: Abacus is an ancient tool used for calculation. Scientific research has proved that the use of Abacus stimulates the nerves in the fingers and sends message through the neurons which helps in accelerating both the hemispheres of the brain. This continuous process of information transfer energizes the brain.
